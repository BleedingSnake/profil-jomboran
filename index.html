Tantangan dan Solusi

While our Go Green initiative has achieved significant success, we continue to face challenges that require innovative solutions and community commitment.

Key Challenges

  • Limited Resources: As a small rural village, we face budget constraints that sometimes slow the implementation of environmental projects.
  • Agricultural Traditions: Some older farmers remain hesitant to adopt new, more sustainable farming practices due to deeply rooted traditional methods.
  • Waste Disposal Infrastructure: Our remote location makes transportation of recyclable materials to processing centers difficult and costly.
  • Climate Change Impacts: Changing weather patterns have affected local crops and increased the frequency of extreme weather events.
  • Youth Migration: Many young people leave the village for education and employment opportunities in urban areas, reducing our workforce for community projects.

Implemented Solutions

  • Grant Applications: Forming a dedicated team to secure environmental grants and partnerships with NGOs to fund green initiatives.
  • Intergenerational Knowledge Exchange: Creating programs where younger villagers share modern sustainable practices while learning traditional wisdom from elders.
  • Community Recycling Center: Establishing a local processing facility for certain recyclables to reduce transportation needs.
  • Climate-Resilient Agriculture: Testing and implementing crop varieties and farming techniques better suited to changing climate conditions.
  • Remote Work Opportunities: Developing digital infrastructure to allow young residents to pursue careers while remaining in the village.
